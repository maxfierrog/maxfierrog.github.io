Knowledge Distillation and Policy Abstraction for Interpretability

January 9, 2025
  Table of contents

Abstract

In reinforcement learning (RL), policy functions are often encoded in non-interpretable functional forms. In particular, the success deep neural netoworks (DNNs) as policy functions has reduced the problem of mechanistic RL agent interpretability to that of DNNs. Taking note of this, we present an approach to RL agent interpretability based on distillation of policy functions into interpretable forms in conjunction with state abstraction onto interpretable domains.
